Popular world singers and bands

Maneskin

Måneskin is an Italian rock band from Rome, consisting of vocalist Damiano David, bassist Victoria De Angelis, guitarist Thomas Raggi and drummer Ethan Torcchio

I Monster

I Monster are an English electronic music duo consisting of Sheffield record producers Dean Honer and Jarrod Gosling. I Monster and his label Twins of Evil are named after horror films starring Peter Cushing

Capital Cities

Capital Cities is an American pop duo from Los Angeles, California, formed in 2008 by Ryan Merchant and Sebu Simonian

Manu Chao

Rantz performer, musician of Spanish origin. Performs songs in several languages ​​- mainly Spanish and French in a combination of rock, reggae and ethnic music styles

Le Tigre

Le Tigre is an American electronic rock band formed by Kathleen Hanna, Joanna Feitman and Sadie Benning in 1998 in New York City. Benning left in 2000 and was replaced by JD Samson for the rest of the band's existence

Eminem

Marshall Bruce Mathers III, better known by his stage name Eminem, is an American rapper, music producer, composer and actor

Fitz and the Tantrums

Fitz and the Tantrums are an American indie pop and neo soul band from Los Angeles, California, that formed in 2008. The band consists of Michael Fitzpatrick, Noelle Scaggs, James King, Joseph Karnes, and Jeremy Ruzumna

Tally Hall

Tally Hall consists of five members, each with a different tie color: Rob Cantor (yellow), Joe Hawley (red), Ross Federman (gray), Andrew Horowitz (green) and Zubin Seji (blue)

Arctic Monkeys

Arctic Monkeys is a British rock band formed in 2002 in the suburbs of Sheffield. The musicians of the band combine different styles and directions of music in their work, among which the main ones are indie rock, psychedelic rock, garage rock and post-punk.

Lemon Demon

Lemon Demon is a music project and band formed by American comedian and musician Neil Sichirega in 2003 in Boston, Massachusetts. Lemon Demon studio work is done exclusively by Cicierega, who is the only official member of the project.

David Kushner

David Kushner is an American singer and songwriter. Raised in the suburbs of Chicago, he moved to Los Angeles and began writing music.

Kiss

Kiss is an American rock band formed in January 1973 in New York by Paul Stanley, Gene Simmons, Ace Fraley, Peter Criss and Brian Stevenson. The collective is primarily known for its members' signature make-up and spectacular concert performances
